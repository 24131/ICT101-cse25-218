Group Work Reflection

Working in a group this semester allowed me to apply Tuckman’s stages of group development. During the Forming stage, our team was polite but unsure of roles. And members were not comfortable around each other and were had to exchange contact details to form a whatsapp group to we can communicate regarding the presentation. We experienced some Storming when we disagreed on task allocation, because no one wanted to take the role of a chairperon and secretary but we resolved it through open discussions. As we moved into the Norming stage, we set clear expectations and improved coordination.And the group members became cormfortable aroud each other and roles became more clear because each member choose their roles and we developed a sense of harmony In the Performing stage, everyone contributed effectively and completed our project successfully. Finally, during Adjourning, we reflected on our collaboration and thanked and appreciated our teamwork. I learned that communication, respect, and shared responsibility are key to successful group work.
