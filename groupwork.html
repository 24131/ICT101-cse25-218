Group Work Reflection

Working in a group this semester allowed me to apply Tuckman’s stages of group development. During the Forming stage, our team was polite but unsure of roles. We experienced some Storming when we disagreed on task allocation, but we resolved it through open discussions. As we moved into the Norming stage, we set clear expectations and improved coordination. In the Performing stage, everyone contributed effectively and completed our project successfully. Finally, during Adjourning, we reflected on our collaboration and celebrated our teamwork. I learned that communication, respect, and shared responsibility are key to successful group work.
